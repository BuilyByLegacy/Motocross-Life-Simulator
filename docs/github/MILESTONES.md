# Recommended Milestones

Milestones group focused delivery goals. They should have clear exit criteria and should not become catch-all backlogs.

## 1. Repository Organization

**Goal:** Make issues, labels, templates, and project workflows consistent.

**Exit criteria:** Issue templates are available, labels are documented, and contributors know how to write actionable issues.

## 2. Design Foundation

**Goal:** Align core principles, terminology, and system boundaries.

**Exit criteria:** Major epics are defined and open questions are captured.

## 3. Simulation Core

**Goal:** Clarify state, time, events, world progression, and persistence.

**Exit criteria:** Core engine responsibilities and integration points are implementation-ready.

## 4. Family Career Loop

**Goal:** Define the emotional and economic loop for household motocross progression.

**Exit criteria:** Family, budget, calendar, race planning, and relationship tradeoffs are documented.

## 5. Racing & Progression

**Goal:** Define race weekends, competition outcomes, rider growth, and career paths.

**Exit criteria:** Competition rules and progression gates are testable and scoped for MVP.

## 6. Assets, Garage & Marketplace

**Goal:** Define ownership, maintenance, purchasing, history, and property interactions.

**Exit criteria:** Asset lifecycle and marketplace flows are clear enough to implement.

## 7. Phone & World UI

**Goal:** Define how the player interacts with messages, calendar, marketplace, race pages, and memories.

**Exit criteria:** MVP screens, notifications, and content responsibilities are documented.

## 8. MVP Vertical Slice

**Goal:** Build the smallest playable slice that proves the game promise.

**Exit criteria:** Included systems, excluded scope, success criteria, and manual test flows are agreed.

## 9. Release Readiness / App Store Version 1.0

**Goal:** Prepare Motocross: Chasing the Dream for TestFlight, App Store review, public launch, and launch-week monitoring.

**Exit criteria:** Version 1.0 launch scope is agreed, launch blockers are tracked, TestFlight plan exists, core career loop and save/load are verified, App Store assets and privacy/compliance requirements are documented, the final launch checklist is complete, and post-launch monitoring is planned.
